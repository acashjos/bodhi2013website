INCEPTION- Idea Impact Presentation

DESCRIPTION:

A two stage competition in which the students need to come with an idea and present it in front of the audience. Idea can be of the various themes given below. The idea can be a working model, a message, an innovative product or an academic project work (All branches of engineering, science & management). You don’t need to come with a completed model, just the blue print of the model is sufficient. Special weightage will be given for completed models and demonstration. Presentation should include Introduction, Background of problem/social concern, Execution Plan, Expected Result (Short Term & Long Term). Time for presentation will be 5 to 6 minutes. Power point presentation can also be done. The idea must be written in a paper format (2000 words max).  It’s a team event with maximum 4 members in a team. There may be a second round depending on the number of participants. Second round will be a spot presentation. A particular theme will be given to the second round participants. They had to develop some idea based on the given theme within a given time interval. Link to download sample ideas and papers will be given in Bodhi 2013 website.
